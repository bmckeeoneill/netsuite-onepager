NETSUITE MODULE REFERENCE — Use this to write accurate, specific solution names and descriptions.
When naming a NetSuite solution, use the exact module name below. Never invent module names.

=== CRM ===
NetSuite CRM — Manages the complete customer lifecycle: sales force automation, marketing automation, customer support, partner management. Unified with ERP and ecommerce.
NetSuite Contract Renewals — Automates subscription and contract renewal workflows, increases retention, reduces manual renewal tracking.
NetSuite Dunning Letters — Automates collections communications, sends payment reminders at configurable intervals based on balance, invoice amount, or aging buckets. Improves cash flow.

=== FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT ===
NetSuite Advanced Financials — Automates budgeting, expense allocations, amortization schedules, and flexible billing. Real-time budget vs. actual visibility.
NetSuite Advanced Revenue Management (ARM) — ASC 606 / IFRS 15 compliant revenue recognition. Automates multi-element arrangement allocation, deferred revenue waterfall, and SSP analysis.
NetSuite AP Automation — Automates invoice capture, approval routing, and payment execution. Includes embedded banking via HSBC for direct bill pay inside NetSuite.
NetSuite Electronic Bank Payments — Automates EFT/ACH payments and collections globally. Prevents check fraud. Single payment management solution across entities.
NetSuite Fixed Asset Management — Tracks fixed assets from acquisition through retirement. Automates depreciation schedules, eliminates spreadsheet-based asset tracking.
NetSuite Multi-Book Accounting — Concurrent reporting under multiple accounting standards (GAAP, IFRS, local GAAP) for multi-entity or international businesses.
NetSuite Planning and Budgeting (NSPB) — Driver-based planning, budgeting, and forecasting with scenario modeling and dashboards. Replaces Excel-based FP&A processes.
NetSuite SuiteBilling — Subscription billing, usage billing, and recurring revenue management. Automates invoicing and integrates with revenue recognition.

=== MANUFACTURING ===
NetSuite Manufacturing Execution (MES) — Paperless shop floor management. Real-time work order visibility, labor tracking, and production reporting accessible from any device.
NetSuite WIP and Routings — Defines manufacturing routings, work centers, labor and machine time, and cost estimates. Full WIP tracking and variance reporting.
NetSuite Work Orders and Assemblies — Manages BOMs, assembly builds, component back-flushing, and multi-level production orders in a unified platform.

=== ECOMMERCE ===
NetSuite SuiteCommerce — Multi-tenant SaaS ecommerce storefront natively integrated with NetSuite ERP. Single platform for B2C or B2B online commerce.
NetSuite SuiteCommerce Advanced (SCA) — Fully customizable ecommerce platform for complex B2B or B2C requirements. Unified with ERP, inventory, and CRM.
NetSuite SuiteCommerce InStore — Cloud-based POS that unifies physical and digital commerce. Enables omnichannel selling from a single platform.
NetSuite Connector — Bi-directional data sync between NetSuite and ecommerce platforms (Shopify, Magento, Amazon, etc.), POS systems, and 3PLs. Eliminates manual data entry.

=== INVENTORY & ORDER MANAGEMENT ===
NetSuite Advanced Inventory — Multi-location inventory optimization, reorder point automation, lot/serial tracking, and real-time availability dashboards.
NetSuite Advanced Order Management — Omnichannel order orchestration with global inventory visibility. Automates order routing and fulfillment based on business rules.
NetSuite Order Management — Flexible order fulfillment with rules-based routing across locations, channels, and fulfillment partners.
NetSuite Production Scheduling — Constraint-based production scheduling that creates realistic schedules to hit delivery dates at optimal cost.
NetSuite Ship Central — Mobile pick, pack, and ship application. Eliminates manual shipping processes and accelerates outbound fulfillment.

=== SUPPLY CHAIN ===
NetSuite Demand Planning — Forecasts required inventory using historical demand and sales projections. Reduces stockouts, excess inventory, and lead time surprises.
NetSuite Procurement — Source-to-pay process management. Vendor relationship management, PO automation, and spend controls.
NetSuite Quality Management — Inspection and testing workflows with pass/fail rules, non-conformance tracking, and rework management. Supports lot-level quality holds.
NetSuite WMS — Warehouse management including directed putaway, pick strategies, bin management, cycle counting, and labor productivity tracking.

=== DATA & ANALYTICS ===
NetSuite SuiteAnalytics — Native BI with saved searches, report builder, and workbooks. Real-time dashboards and KPI visibility without exporting to Excel.
NetSuite Product Data Management — Centralizes product engineering data (specs, BOMs, drawings) accessible across departments without manual handoffs.

=== HUMAN CAPITAL MANAGEMENT ===
NetSuite SuitePeople HR — Core HR system integrated with NetSuite ERP. Org chart, employee records, onboarding, time-off management, and workforce reporting.
NetSuite SuitePeople Payroll — Full-service US payroll processing natively inside NetSuite. Eliminates payroll export/import and reconciliation errors.
SuitePeople Performance Management — Goal setting, performance reviews, and employee development tracking tied to workforce data in NetSuite.
NetSuite Incentive Compensation — Sales commission plan design, quota tracking, and commission payment processing. Eliminates spreadsheet-based comp management.

=== PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AUTOMATION (PSA) ===
NetSuite OpenAir — Standalone PSA for project management, resource planning, time/expense capture, and services financials. Strong for large services organizations.
NetSuite SuiteProjects — Native PSA built on NetSuite. Bid-to-bill project management, resource designation, project-based financials, and utilization reporting.
NetSuite Project Management — Gantt chart project tracking, KPI dashboards, and mobile access. Best for project visibility within a broader NetSuite implementation.

=== ADDITIONAL MODULES ===
NetSuite OneWorld — Multi-entity, multi-currency, multi-subsidiary management. Local tax compliance, intercompany transactions, and consolidated global reporting.
NetSuite CPQ — Configure, Price, Quote automation. Guided product configuration, dynamic pricing rules, and quote generation. Reduces quoting errors and sales cycle time.
NetSuite Field Service Management (NextService) — Scheduling, dispatch, mobile work orders, and field technician management for service-based businesses.
